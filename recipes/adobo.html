Chicken Adobo

What is chicken adobo?

Chicken adobo is a classic Filipino dish made by marinating chicken in soy sauce, white vinegar and spices. The chicken is simmered in the sauce, making is tender and intensely flavourful. My mama prefers to pan-fry the chicken until the sauce dries up, leaving us with sweet, sticky and slightly tangy chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (thighs, legs or breasts can be used)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves crushed garlic
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1 tbsp black peppercorns
  • 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan and pour the vegetable oil in it.
  2. When the oil is hot enough, add the chicken and pan fry until it is slightly brown.
  3. Add 1/2 cup of water, bay leaves, whole peppercorn and crushed garlic. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Add vinegar and let it boil. Stir and cook for 10 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Add soy sauce and continue to let it boil until most of the sauce dries out, and the chicken is covered with a sticky glaze.
  6. Serve with white rice and enjoy!
