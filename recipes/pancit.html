Pancit Bihon

Pancit Bihon is a Filipino stir-fried dish that usually consists of vermicelli noodles cooked with soy sauce, chopped vegetables, meat and/or seafood. For my Mama's version, I'll be using shredded chicken with carrots, cabbage and snow peas. She also likes adding chorizo bits to give it that extra flavour and colour.

Ingredients

  • 1 1b rice (vermicelli) noodles
  • 2 chicken breasts cooked, deboned and shredded
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 1 carrot julienned
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 medium-sized onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 tbsps soy sauce
  • 3 to 4 cups of water or chicken stock

Steps

  1. In a large wok, saute the garlic and onion.
  2. Add the chicken, season with salt and black pepper, and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and let it cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add carrots and snow peas and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add water or chicken stock and let it boil. Then add the rice noodles and set the heat to the lowest setting to allow the noodles to cook slowly.
  6. Once the noodles are cooked, add cabbage and cook until most of the broth has evaporated.
  7. Add chorizo and cook for about 5 minutes.
  8. Serve with calamansi or lemon and enjoy!
